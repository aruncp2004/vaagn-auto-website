What is a Battery Rickshaw?

A battery rickshaw, or e-rickshaw, is a three-wheeled vehicle powered by an electric battery. Designed to be eco-friendly, economical, and ideal for last-mile transport, they’re increasingly replacing fuel-powered autos. Compared to traditional rickshaws: ✔ Lower operating cost ✔ Zero tailpipe emissions ✔ Ideal for Chennai’s urban roads
