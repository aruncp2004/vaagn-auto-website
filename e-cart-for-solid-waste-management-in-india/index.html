What is an E-Cart for Solid Waste Management? 

An E-Cart, or electric cart, is a battery-operated vehicle designed to collect and transport solid waste. Unlike traditional diesel vehicles, E-Carts are emission-free, operate at lower costs, and can navigate narrow city lanes with ease — making them ideal for waste collection in both urban and semi-urban areas. 

Why India Needs E-Carts Now More Than Ever 

India generates over 150,000 tonnes of municipal solid waste every day. Conventional collection systems contribute significantly to pollution and traffic congestion. With increasing environmental awareness and government initiatives like Swachh Bharat Abhiyan and FAME India, E-Carts offer a futuristic and sustainable approach to managing waste efficiently.
