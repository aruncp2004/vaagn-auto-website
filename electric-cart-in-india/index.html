What is an Electric Cart? 

An electric cart is a battery-powered vehicle designed for short-distance transportation. Unlike traditional fuel-powered carts, electric carts produce zero emissions, run quietly, and require minimal maintenance. They’re commonly used in:

  • Airports & railway stations
  • Golf courses
  • Hotels & resorts
  • Industrial warehouses
  • Residential campuses
  • Eco-tourism zones

The Rise of Electric Carts in India

India is embracing electric mobility faster than ever, driven by the need to cut carbon emissions, reduce fuel dependency, and promote clean energy. Government initiatives such as FAME India Scheme and the push toward EV adoption have created fertile ground for electric cart manufacturers to thrive. As industries seek green alternatives, electric carts are becoming a go-to solution, especially in urban and semi-urban areas.
