Our Story

From Sriperumbudur to India's roads

Vaagn Auto was founded with one conviction — India's commercial operators deserved better. Petrol and diesel vehicles were costing operators lakhs every year in fuel and maintenance. Electric options existed, but none were engineered for the payload demands, road conditions, and economics of Indian commercial transport.

So we built them ourselves. Starting from our factory in Sriperumbudur, Tamil Nadu, we engineered a fleet of electric commercial vehicles from the ground up — designed to earn their keep from day one.
