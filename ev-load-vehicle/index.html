🚚 What is an EV Load Vehicle?

An EV load vehicle, also called an electric cargo rickshaw or electric loader, is a battery-operated three-wheeler designed to transport goods over short to medium distances. Built for sustainability and low-cost operations, these electric vehicles are ideal for urban delivery, retail distribution, agriculture, and small-scale logistics.
