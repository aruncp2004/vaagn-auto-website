What is an E-Auto? 

An E-Auto is a three-wheeled electric vehicle designed for passenger or cargo transport. Unlike conventional auto-rickshaws powered by petrol, diesel, or CNG, E-Autos run on electric batteries, producing zero tailpipe emissions. They are quieter, more cost-efficient, and contribute to a cleaner urban environment. 

Why E-Autos Are Gaining Traction in India 

India, home to over 2 million auto-rickshaws, is one of the largest markets for E-Autos. Here's why the shift is accelerating: 

  • Cost Savings: Low running and maintenance costs. 
  • Government Incentives: Subsidies under FAME II and state EV policies. 
  • Environmental Benefits: Reduction in CO₂ and air pollution. 
  • Urban Mobility: Ideal for last-mile connectivity and shared transport.
