What is an Electric Auto Tipper?

An electric auto tipper is a compact, battery-powered three-wheeled vehicle designed for door-to-door garbage collection in urban and semi-urban areas. Unlike diesel vehicles, they are quiet, emission-free, and more economical to operate and maintain.

Perfect for narrow lanes and crowded neighborhoods, these vehicles feature a tipping mechanism for safe and hygienic waste unloading.
