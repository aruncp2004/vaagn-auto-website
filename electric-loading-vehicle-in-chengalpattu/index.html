What is an Electric Loading Vehicle?

An Electric Loading Vehicle (ELV) is a battery-powered cargo vehicle designed to carry goods over short to medium distances. Unlike conventional diesel or petrol vehicles, these are powered by rechargeable lithium-ion or lead-acid batteries, offering zero emissions and low operational costs. In a city like Chengalpattu, known for its industrial growth, warehouses, and small business hubs, ELVs have emerged as the smart choice for last-mile delivery and intra-city logistics.
