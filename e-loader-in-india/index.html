What is an E-Loader?

An E-Loader is a compact, battery-powered cargo vehicle used for transporting goods over short distances. Perfect for congested city streets and semi-urban routes, these electric loaders offer zero-emission performance, making them a smart and sustainable replacement for traditional fuel-based delivery vehicles.
