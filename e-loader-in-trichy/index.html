What is an E-Loader?

An E-Loader (Electric Loader) is a battery-powered three-wheeled vehicle used primarily for transporting goods. With zero emissions and lower operational costs, it has become an ideal alternative to traditional petrol or diesel cargo vehicles—especially in cities like Trichy that are pushing toward sustainability.
