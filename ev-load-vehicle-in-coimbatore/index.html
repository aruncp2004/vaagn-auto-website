1. Regular Battery Check and Charging Routine 

The battery is the heart of your EV load vehicle. In Coimbatore’s varying climate conditions from hot summers to monsoon rains—it’s essential to ensure the battery stays in top shape. 

Maintenance Tips: 

  • Charge after every trip or once the charge drops below 20%. 
  • Avoid full discharge; lithium-ion batteries last longer when charged regularly. 
  • Use the recommended charger provided by Vaagn. 
  • Ensure the charging point is moisture-free and properly earthed. 

Pro Tip: Don’t overcharge. Remove the charger once the battery hits 100% to avoid overheating. 

2. Keep the EV Clean and Dry 

Coimbatore's climate includes dust and humidity, which can affect your vehicle’s electrical components. 

Best Practices: 

  • Clean your EV load vehicle weekly using a dry cloth and mild cleaning solution. 
  • Avoid spraying water directly on the battery or motor components. 
  • During monsoon, park your vehicle in a covered or elevated area to prevent water ingress. 

3. Inspect Tyres and Brakes Weekly 

Load vehicles experience high weight-bearing stress, especially when operating in industrial zones or hill routes in and around Coimbatore like Marudhamalai or Perur. 

Routine Checks: 

  • Ensure tyre pressure is at the manufacturer-recommended level. 
  • Look for uneven wear patterns—this may signal suspension issues. 
  • Inspect brake pads monthly and replace if worn out. 

Tip from Vaagn: Tyres that are well-inflated help save battery power and improve ride comfort. 

 4. Monitor Motor and Controller Health 

The motor and controller are responsible for propelling the vehicle and managing power efficiently. These components must stay cool and dust-free. 

What You Can Do: 

  • Check for unusual sounds or vibrations when accelerating. 
  • If performance drops or the vehicle overheats, visit a Vaagn service center immediately. 
  • Avoid steep inclines with a fully loaded vehicle, unless your model is specifically designed for it. 

5. Update Software and Controller Settings 

Modern EVs, including Vaagn’s load vehicles, often come with smart controller units that manage battery usage and driving modes. 

Software Maintenance: 

  • Visit an authorized Vaagn service center in Coimbatore every 3 months. 
  • Get the latest firmware updates that improve energy efficiency and motor response. 

6. Follow a Scheduled Service Plan 

Just because EVs have fewer moving parts doesn’t mean they don’t need servicing. A regular check-up ensures small issues don’t turn into big problems. 

Service Checklist Includes: 

  • Battery health report 
  • Motor efficiency test 
  • Controller diagnostics 
  • Tyre alignment and brake pad check 
  • Overall electrical wiring inspection 

7. Use Original Spare Parts 

Using non-genuine parts can damage your EV's performance and may void the warranty. 

Recommendation: Always use Vaagn-certified parts for replacements to ensure compatibility and reliability.
